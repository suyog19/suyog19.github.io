The learning model

How the learning experience works

Each course publishes its confirmed schedule, support policy, and completion criteria separately.

Learn live

Instructor-led online cohorts combine explanation, demonstration, and guided practice.

Practise between sessions

Short tasks apply each concept before the next part of learning.

Receive review and support

Selected submission reviews, doubt clearing, or office hours are provided according to each course policy.

Build something complete

An applied project or capstone demonstrates a useful, reviewable capability.
