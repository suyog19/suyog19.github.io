Privacy Notice

What is collected, why it is needed, who supports the service and how to exercise your rights.

Data and purposesProvidersRetentionYour rights

Data and purposes

  • Interest and communication: contact details, course choice, message and consent evidence to answer requests and send requested updates.
  • Application and enrolment: identity, email, timezone, adult confirmation, readiness answers, references and acknowledgements to authenticate, review and administer an application.
  • Payment: transaction identifiers, amount, status, invoice/refund and reconciliation records when payment later opens. Card details, bank credentials, CVV and UPI PIN are not stored by Suyog Joshi or Software Signal.
  • Learning and completion: attendance, submissions, support activity, access and completion evidence when a course runs.
  • Site and security: analytics, browser storage, session, audit and limited operational logs to understand public-page use, maintain sessions, prevent misuse and diagnose failures.

Service providers

GitHub Pages hosts the public site; Google Analytics measures public-page use; AWS services support authentication, application storage, email and operations. Razorpay may process future payments. Meeting, storage and learning-resource providers such as Microsoft Teams, OneDrive and GitHub are disclosed before use for a cohort.

Retention

  • Abandoned local browser drafts: up to 7 days or until submission.
  • Declined, withdrawn or superseded readiness answers: delete or anonymise after 12 months unless needed for a dispute or legal obligation.
  • Active learner records: for the learner relationship, then reviewed for deletion or anonymisation at closure.
  • Required acknowledgement and audit evidence: 3 years after the related application or learner relationship ends.
  • Communication delivery metadata and support records: normally 12 months, longer only for an active matter.
  • Payment and invoice records: for the applicable financial, fraud-prevention, dispute and legal period.
  • Recordings and completion records: the cohort notice states the period before enrolment; none currently exist.

Access, correction and deletion

Email contact@suyogjoshi.com to request access, correction or deletion. Requests are acknowledged within 2 business days and receive a substantive response or progress update within 7 business days. Some minimal financial, fraud-prevention, dispute or legal records cannot be deleted immediately; the response will explain why.

Optional consent

Marketing, testimonials and public or promotional use are optional and separate from application, enrolment and course access. Declining or withdrawing them does not affect admission.
