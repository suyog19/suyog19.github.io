Privacy

The application process is designed to collect only the identity, profile, eligibility, acknowledgement, readiness, application, audit, and communication information needed to operate and support an application. Do not submit sensitive or unnecessary information. Access is restricted to the learner, authorised administrators, and platform processors needed to provide the service.

Operational retention follows a purpose-limited schedule pending qualified review. Privacy and closure requests may be sent to contact@suyogjoshi.com.

Dates and delivery details are not yet confirmed. A cohort will run only after its published minimum and operational conditions are met. Any recording availability, access period, participant notice, privacy control, and replacement or revocation terms will be published before applications open.

Any future payment, tax, cancellation, or refund terms require separate approval and qualified advice before payment is enabled.

Conduct and confidentiality

Learners will be expected to participate respectfully, protect other participants' personal or confidential information, and avoid unlawful, abusive, deceptive, or disruptive use of course spaces and materials. Detailed conduct rules will be published before enrolment.

Support and grievance process

Email contact@suyogjoshi.com for support, privacy requests, or grievances. The response targets are acknowledgement within 2 business days and a substantive response or progress update within 7 business days.
