Terms of Enrolment

The conditions that apply when you apply for and join Software Signal training.

EligibilityAdmissionCertificatesMaterials and learner workAccepted version

Eligibility and accurate information

You must be 18 or older. Provide accurate, current information and do not include sensitive or unnecessary personal data. Access is personal and non-transferable.

Application and admission

Applying is free. Admission is reviewed and discretionary; an application does not reserve a seat. No placement, employment, salary, income, certification or particular learning outcome is guaranteed. Course dates remain tentative until a cohort is expressly confirmed.

A cohort runs only when its published minimum cohort size is reached and the provider confirms that instructor availability, delivery systems and learner-support arrangements are ready. If these conditions are not met, no payment is requested.

Certificates

No certificate is currently promised because completion criteria have not been published. If a course later offers one, its offer will state the attendance, assessment or project requirements and deadline. Any certificate is a course-completion record, not a recognised qualification or external accreditation.

Course materials, licences and learner work

  • Provided materials and recordings are for personal, non-transferable course use.
  • Do not redistribute, resell or publicly post proprietary materials, recordings, exercises or solution sets.
  • You retain ownership of original code and project work you create, subject to third-party and open-source licences.
  • You may reuse your knowledge and original work after the course.
  • Starter code, repositories, datasets, libraries and third-party resources remain governed by their stated licences.
  • Identifiable submissions, testimonials, recordings or examples are not used publicly or promotionally without separate permission.
  • Anonymised examples may be used in teaching only after identifying details are removed.

Your accepted version

The policy version shown with an application or offer is recorded with that acknowledgement. Terms accepted with an offer govern that confirmed enrolment and are not silently replaced later, except where legally required or expressly agreed.
