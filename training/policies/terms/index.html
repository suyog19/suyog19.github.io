Eligibility and accurate information

You must be 18 or older. Provide accurate, current information and do not include sensitive or unnecessary personal data. Access and offers are personal and non-transferable except through the approved transfer process.

Application and admission

Applying is free. Admission is reviewed and discretionary; an application does not reserve a seat. An offer states the course, cohort, total price, deposit, remaining fee, deadlines and immutable policy versions. A seat is reserved only after the service confirms the required deposit. No placement, employment, salary, income, certification or particular learning outcome is guaranteed.

A cohort runs only when its published minimum cohort size is reached and the provider confirms instructor availability, delivery systems and learner-support readiness. If the provider cancels or the cohort is not formed, the refundable amount is returned under the Cancellation and Refund Policy.

Fees, payment and confirmation

  • Python Foundations for Data Science: INR 4,000 total, INR 1,000 deposit and INR 3,000 remaining fee.
  • Applied Data Analysis with Python: INR 8,000 total, INR 2,000 deposit and INR 6,000 remaining fee.
  • Prices include applicable taxes and payments are INR only. International and partial Payment Link payments are disabled.
  • The deposit and remaining-fee deadlines are each 7 calendar days. There is no automatic balance grace period.
  • A Payment Link expires at its deposit deadline. A technical pre-expiry replacement keeps the original deadline; an approved post-expiry replacement creates a new 7-day window.
  • A return from Razorpay is not proof of payment. The learner journey must show a confirmed status before another payment is attempted.
  • The production record is a payment confirmation, not a tax invoice. It retains its unique confirmation identifier and uses the authoritative system issue time.

Razorpay-owned email, SMS and reminders are disabled. Software Signal may send transactional status messages through its own platform.

Course delivery

software-signal-course-delivery@1.1.0 governs published schedules and delivery. Cohort dates, session schedule, timezone, delivery tools and available support are shown before enrolment. Reasonable operational changes may be made with learner notice. A material provider cancellation follows the refund policy.

Joining information may be communicated after payment and enrolment confirmation, but access to course resources remains subject to the separately controlled access stage. Gate 4 access is not promised by a payment confirmation alone.

Certificates

No certificate entitlement is promised until completion criteria are separately published. No attendance or assessment minimum applies unless it is published with the offer. A future certificate would be a course-completion record, not a recognised qualification or external accreditation.

Your accepted version
