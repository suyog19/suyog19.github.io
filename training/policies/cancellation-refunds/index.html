Cancellation and Refund Policy

Deterministic outcomes for the current stage, when applications and payment are closed.

Current payment boundary

No application, deposit, remaining fee or tax is collected. Therefore no gateway deduction, invoice, receipt or refund-processing period currently arises. A future paid offer must present its versioned cancellation, refund and transfer rules before payment.

Fees, taxes and records

Published course fees are informational while payment is closed. No gateway charge is deducted. No tax invoice or payment receipt is issued without a transaction. Any future payment page must show whether taxes are included, applicable deductions, the refund-processing target and how an invoice or receipt is provided.

Version binding

Policy 1.1.0 governs only the current no-payment stage. Before any future payment, the offer and checkout must identify the applicable commercial-policy version. Accepting a later offer binds that disclosed version; it is not silently changed after payment.
