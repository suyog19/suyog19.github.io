Who provides this site and training

This website and Software Signal training are provided by Suyog Joshi, trading as Software Signal, in Pune, Maharashtra, India.

Information collected and why

  • Website use: page, browser, device and approximate location information for aggregate analytics, security and site improvement.
  • Contact and feedback: name, email, message, anonymous feedback identifier, rating and optional comment to answer enquiries, prevent spam and improve content.
  • Application and profile: identity, email, timezone, adult confirmation, readiness answers, references and versioned acknowledgements to authenticate, review and administer an application.
  • Payment and reconciliation: provider and internal transaction identifiers, amount, status, confirmation, refund, dispute and reconciliation records. Software Signal does not store card details, bank credentials, CVV or UPI PIN.
  • Learning: attendance, submissions, support activity, access and completion evidence when a course runs.
  • Security and operations: session, audit and limited operational logs to maintain access, prevent misuse, investigate failures and preserve reliable records.

Do not send sensitive or unnecessary personal information through free-text fields.

Service providers and storage

GitHub Pages hosts the public website. Google Analytics measures public-page use. AWS services support authentication, forms, application storage, email and operations. Meeting, storage and learning-resource providers are disclosed before use for a cohort.

Razorpay

Razorpay processes available INR payments as an independent payment service provider and may receive contact information, payment amount, course/payment description, transaction identifiers, device and fraud-prevention data. Razorpay applies its own privacy and security terms. Software Signal receives payment status and reconciliation data but not card numbers, CVV, bank passwords or UPI PIN. Razorpay-owned email, SMS and reminders are disabled; your bank or payment app may still send its own transactional messages.

Cookies and browser storage

Google Analytics may use browser identifiers for site measurement. The site also uses browser storage for navigation state, anonymous feedback identity, application drafts and authenticated learner sessions. Disabling storage may prevent those features from working correctly.

How long information is retained

  • Abandoned application drafts stored in your browser: up to 7 days or until submission.
  • Applications, learner profiles and unsuccessful applications: 365 days.
  • Payment, refund, reconciliation, dispute, support, grievance and audit evidence: at least 365 days, and longer while required for an active dispute, legal requirement, security investigation or reconciliation.
  • Course recordings: no more than 90 days.
  • Operational security logs and learning records: retained only as long as reasonably required for the stated operational, learning, security or legal purpose.

Optional choices

Marketing, testimonials and identifiable public or promotional use are optional and separate from application, enrolment and course access. Declining or withdrawing optional consent does not affect admission.

Access, correction and deletion

You may request access to, correction of or deletion of your information. Identity may need to be verified. Eligible personal data is deleted or anonymised. Required financial, audit, fraud-prevention, dispute, reconciliation, security-investigation or legal evidence is preserved, and the response will explain what remains and why.
