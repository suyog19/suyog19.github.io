Software Systems

AI-native Learning Platform

Exploring how structured content and AI can support deeper technical learning.

Context

This is an early exploration into how learning systems can evolve when content, structure, and AI assistance are combined.

The focus is on depth of understanding rather than surface-level content consumption.

Problem

Most technical learning platforms focus on content delivery, not understanding.

The problem was to explore how structure and AI could help learners go deeper into concepts.

Approach

The approach combines structured content, conceptual layering, and AI assistance to support deeper understanding.

The system is evolving and not yet fully defined.

What worked

The concept shows promise in improving how learners engage with technical material.

It enables more structured exploration of ideas.

What didn't

The system is still in early stages and lacks real user feedback.

Many assumptions are yet to be validated.

What changed

It shifted my thinking toward learning systems that prioritize depth over volume.
